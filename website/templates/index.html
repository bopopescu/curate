How Curate.io Works

Once you sign up for a Curate.io account, you can start automating scheduled newsletters to your client's email list. Based on your industry, we curate the most relevant and engaging content to populate your newsletter. Optionally, you can add custom content to your newsletters before they go out, but otherwise, we take care of everything.

No 'Management' Portals

When you are busy running a business, the last thing you want to do is learn how to use another piece of software. Curate.io has worked tirelessly to design a system that provides only optional tools so you can stay in contact with your clients effortlessly.

Better Content

Our informative emails use machine learning technology to pick the most interesting content. We scour the web for interesting tidbits, approved by professionals, when designing each email. This helps us maximize the efficacy and engagement from your clients so you can increase loyalty and help your clients learn.

Give-A-Ways, Discounts, and More

Our emails feature a host of awesome features that separate ourselves from your typical boring newsletters. Monthly give-a-ways help clients feel rewarded, industry topics help clients learn and stay healthy, and discounts help clients save on industry specific products.
